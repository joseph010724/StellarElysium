The 12 Zodiac Signs Profiles


ARIESmore_vert
Aries (March 21 - April 19)close

Aries, the first sign of the zodiac, is known for its energetic and adventurous spirit. Governed by Mars, the planet of action and aggression, Aries individuals are confident, courageous, and eager to take on new challenges. They are natural leaders, often pioneering new ideas and ventures. Their impulsive nature can sometimes lead to hasty decisions, but their enthusiasm and determination usually drive them toward success.

TAURUSmore_vert
Taurus (April 20 - May 20)close

Taurus, ruled by Venus, is an earth sign known for its practical and dependable nature. Taureans are often associated with a love for comfort, luxury, and stability. They are reliable and patient, taking a steady, methodical approach to life and its challenges. Although they are typically calm and peaceful, when their values or security are threatened, they can become stubborn and resistant to change.

GEMINImore_vert
Gemini (May 21 - June 20)close

Gemini is an air sign ruled by Mercury, the planet of communication. Known for their adaptability and curiosity, Geminis are social butterflies who enjoy connecting with others and exploring new ideas. They are quick-witted and versatile, able to see multiple perspectives and enjoy variety in all aspects of life. However, their dual nature can sometimes make them seem inconsistent or indecisive, as they often have conflicting desires.

CANCERmore_vert
Cancer (June 21 - July 22)close

Cancer, ruled by the Moon, is a water sign characterized by its nurturing and emotional nature. Those born under this sign are deeply connected to their family and home, and they are highly empathetic, often taking on the emotional needs of others. Cancers are protective and intuitive, able to sense the emotions of those around them. While they are caring and loving, they can also be sensitive and easily hurt if they feel their emotional bonds are threatened.

LEOmore_vert
Leo (July 23 - August 22)close

Leo, ruled by the Sun, is a fire sign known for its charismatic and confident personality. Leos are natural-born leaders, with a flair for drama and a desire to be admired. They are generous, creative, and enthusiastic, often the center of attention in any social setting. While they are warm-hearted and loyal, they can also be prone to arrogance and a desire for recognition, sometimes coming across as domineering.

VIRGOmore_vert
Virgo (August 23 - September 22)close

Virgo, ruled by Mercury, is an earth sign known for its attention to detail and practical approach to life. Virgos are analytical, hardworking, and often perfectionistic, constantly striving for improvement in themselves and the world around them. They are methodical and reliable, with a strong sense of duty and a desire to help others. While their critical nature helps them solve problems, they can sometimes be overly self-critical or focus too much on minor flaws.

LIBRAmore_vert
Libra (September 23 - October 22)close

Libra, ruled by Venus, is an air sign associated with balance, harmony, and beauty. Libras are diplomatic and fair-minded, always seeking to create peace and understanding in their relationships. They value aesthetics and enjoy the finer things in life, often with a strong sense of style. Libras are social and charming, with a knack for mediation and making others feel at ease. However, their desire to please others can sometimes lead to indecision or avoidance of conflict.

SCORPIOmore_vert
Scorpio (October 23 - November 21)close

Scorpio, ruled by Pluto, is a water sign known for its intensity and depth. Scorpios are passionate, determined, and fiercely loyal, with a strong desire to uncover the truth. They are mysterious and often private, not easily letting others into their inner world. Scorpios have a powerful presence and can be very persuasive and strategic. While they are deeply emotional, they tend to keep their feelings guarded, and their intensity can sometimes come across as intimidating or manipulative.

SAGITTARIUSmore_vert
Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21)close

Sagittarius, ruled by Jupiter, is a fire sign that embodies optimism, adventure, and a love for freedom. Sagittarians are curious, philosophical, and always eager to explore new ideas, places, and cultures. They value independence and honesty, often seeking to broaden their horizons through travel and learning. With their upbeat nature, Sagittarians can inspire others, but their bluntness and desire for freedom can sometimes make them seem tactless or unreliable.

CAPRICORNmore_vert
Capricorn (December 22 - January 19)close

Capricorn, ruled by Saturn, is an earth sign known for its ambition and disciplined approach to life. Capricorns are hard workers with a strong sense of responsibility, always focused on achieving their long-term goals. They are practical, grounded, and methodical, often taking the time to plan and execute their ambitions with precision. While they can be serious and reserved, their dedication to success and stability is what drives them, and they can be counted on in times of need.

AQUARIUSmore_vert
Aquarius (January 20 - February 18)close

Aquarius, ruled by Uranus, is an air sign known for its innovative and independent nature. Aquarians are forward-thinking and value individuality, often ahead of their time with ideas that challenge the status quo. They are intellectuals with a strong sense of humanitarianism, always looking for ways to improve society and make the world a better place. While they can be eccentric and unpredictable, their open-mindedness and originality make them fascinating and unique.

PISCESmore_vert
Pisces (February 19 - March 20)close

Pisces, ruled by Neptune, is a water sign known for its compassion, creativity, and emotional depth. Pisceans are empathetic, intuitive, and sensitive, often deeply attuned to the feelings of others. They are dreamers and artists, with a vivid imagination that often leads them into creative pursuits. While their gentle and caring nature makes them beloved by others, they can also be prone to escapism and emotional overwhelm, sometimes retreating from reality when faced with hardship.
